About Website

The main objective of this website is to facilitate the students who are engaged in the falcon’s institute, providing them information about different educational events along with calendar and programs, which will help them to enhance their skills and gaining valued experience. Information regarding class scheduling, teacher’s information will make student up to date.

As for the students who are not the part of institute can get information about different recent activities and educational news through website or even if they are interested in joining the institute, they can leave their email address subscribing the website and the institute will reach out to them.
